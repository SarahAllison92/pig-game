Game Rules

Each player takes turns rolling the dice, the current score will continue to add up unless you roll a 1 and then the current score will reset and it will be the next players turn. Use the hold button to add your current score to the overall score which will reset your current score and give the next player their turn also. The first person to get their overall score to 100 wins!!
